CONTROLS

Use KEYBOARD ARROWS for moving the farmer and exploring the map:
  • ARROW UP : Go ahead
  • ARROW LEFT : Turn left
  • ARROW RIGHT : Turn right

Use the MOUSE for changing the camera view

There are two buttons:
- RESTART for restarting the game
- MENU for coming back to the homepage

GENERAL RULES

There are two point counters:

- LIFE (shown at right upper corner), starting from 10 points and decreasing of 1 each time a bee stings you

- SCORE (shown at left upper corner), starting from 0 points and increasing of 1 each time you approach a fruit on the ground

HOW QUIT

There are two possible endings:

- if SCORE counter reachs 10 points, then you WIN

- if LIFE counter reachs 0 points OR you fall in a lake, then you LOOSE
